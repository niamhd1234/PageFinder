About PageFinder

Hi! My name is Niamh and I am a second year MINT student at Dublin City University and I am the founder of PageFinder! I decided to mix my love of reading with my (new) found love of coding and have developed a webiste to help you find your next book to read. I was inspired to create this platform as I would find myself wondering around the libray unsure of what books to choose! I felt overwhelmed with the selection and wanted a helping hand to choose! I hope you enjoy!

Our Book of the Month is voted every month accross our social media platforms and we are constantly updating our book reccomondations! If want to see any books featured or write us a book review email founder@pagefinder.ie I look forward to hearing from you!
